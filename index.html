Who We Are

Campus Crusade for Christ (CRU) is an inter­domi­national Christian organization for college and university students. Its main mission is spreading the word of God to college campuses and providing an equal opportunity for all students to hear about how they can form a relationship with God through Jesus Christ. CRU IIT is the CRU branch located at Illinois Institute of Technology. CRU is composed of a team of students and faculty, who have dedicated themselves to CRU’s cause. CRU IIT works with the Chicago Metro Campus Crusade for Christ to spread the word of Christ.
